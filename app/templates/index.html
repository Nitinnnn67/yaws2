About PRUTSIM

Saket Gyanpeeth ventured into the field of management education by establishing “Pramod Ram Ujagar Tiwari Saket Institute of Management-(PRUTSIM)” in the year 2009. The institute offers two years fulltime M.B.A./M.M.S (Master of Management Studies) course affiliated to University of Mumbai and approved by All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE) and Directorate of technical education(D.T.E. –MB. 3158)/ Government of Maharashtra. The institute has an intake capacity of 120 students. The institute has a Hindi linguistic minority status from Government of Maharashtra.
