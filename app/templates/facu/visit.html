Visit Saket College

About Our Campus

Saket College is located in the vibrant city of Kalyan. Our campus spans across a beautiful landscape, offering state-of-the-art facilities for academic and extracurricular activities. We welcome prospective students, parents, alumni, and visitors to explore our campus.

How to Reach

Saket College is easily accessible through various modes of transportation. Located in Kalyan (East), our campus can be reached via local trains, buses, and private vehicles.

By Train

Kalyan Junction is the nearest railway station, located approximately 2 km from the college campus. You can take a local train from CST, Dadar, Thane or any station on the central line to reach Kalyan. From Kalyan station, you can take an auto-rickshaw or bus to reach the college campus.

By Bus

Several NMMT and KDMT buses operate to and from Kalyan. Alight at the Saket College bus stop. The college is just a short walk from the bus stop.

By Road

If you are coming by private vehicle, the college is easily accessible via Saket Vidyanagari Marg. Ample parking space is available inside the campus.

Campus Tour

We offer guided tours of our campus to prospective students and visitors. The tour includes visits to academic buildings, library, laboratories, sports facilities, and other areas of interest. Campus tours are available on weekdays (Monday to Friday) from 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM.

To schedule a campus tour, please contact our administrative office at least two days in advance.
