Visit Prutsim MMS College

About Our Campus

Prutsim MMS College is located in the serene city of Mandya. Our campus provides a perfect blend of modern infrastructure and natural beauty, offering cutting-edge facilities for academic and extracurricular activities. We warmly welcome prospective students, parents, alumni, and visitors to explore our vibrant campus.

Campus Infrastructure

Prutsim MMS College boasts state-of-the-art infrastructure designed to provide students with an exceptional learning environment. Our facilities are thoughtfully planned to promote academic excellence and holistic development.

Advanced Library

Our library is a comprehensive learning resource center with over 30,000 volumes covering various disciplines, digital resources, and research journals. The library features quiet study areas, group discussion rooms, and a digital section with access to online databases and e-journals.

Research Centers

Our specialized research centers are equipped with advanced equipment and technology to support cutting-edge research in various fields. These centers provide students and faculty with opportunities for innovative research and development projects.

Sports Complex

Our comprehensive sports complex includes indoor and outdoor facilities for various sports. We have dedicated courts for basketball, volleyball, and badminton, along with a well-maintained cricket ground and athletic track. Indoor facilities include a modern gymnasium, table tennis arena, and yoga center.

Student Amenities

Our campus features modern amenities including a spacious cafeteria serving nutritious meals, student lounges, medical center, and counseling facilities. We also maintain a dedicated career development center to support students in their professional growth.

Campus Tour

Experience our campus firsthand through our guided tours. Our student ambassadors will show you around our academic facilities, research centers, and recreational areas. Campus tours are available on weekdays from 9:30 AM to 4:00 PM.

Please schedule your tour at least three working days in advance to ensure the best experience.

Location & Directions

PUTSIM College is strategically located at Saket Vidyanagari, Chinchpada Road in Kalyan East, Maharashtra. Our campus is easily accessible by public transport and private vehicles, with excellent connectivity to Mumbai and surrounding areas.
