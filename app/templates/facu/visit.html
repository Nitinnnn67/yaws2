Visit Saket College

About Our Campus

Saket College is located in the vibrant city of Kalyan. Our campus spans across a beautiful landscape, offering state-of-the-art facilities for academic and extracurricular activities. We welcome prospective students, parents, alumni, and visitors to explore our campus.

Campus Infrastructure

Saket College offers world-class infrastructure to provide students with an excellent learning environment. Our facilities are designed to enhance academic growth while supporting extracurricular and recreational activities.

Modern Library

Our spacious, well-equipped library houses over 25,000 books across various disciplines, academic journals, magazines, and digital resources. With dedicated reading zones, discussion areas, and computer terminals for digital research, the library serves as a knowledge hub for students and faculty alike.

Library Reading Area
Book Collections

State-of-the-Art Classrooms

All classrooms are equipped with smart boards, projectors, and modern furniture to create an engaging learning environment. Air-conditioned lecture halls with excellent acoustics ensure comfortable academic sessions throughout the year.

Smart Classroom
Lecture Hall

Modern Gymnasium

Our gymnasium features the latest fitness equipment, weight training section, cardio zone, and dedicated areas for yoga and aerobics. Professional trainers are available to guide students with personalized fitness routines and wellness programs.

Gym Equipment
Fitness Training Area

Sports Facilities

The campus includes outdoor courts for basketball, volleyball, and badminton, along with a cricket ground and football field. Indoor sports facilities include table tennis, chess, and carrom to encourage students' participation in sports activities.

Sports Ground
Indoor Sports Area

Cafeteria

Our spacious cafeteria offers a variety of nutritious and delicious meals, snacks, and beverages at affordable prices. With ample seating capacity and a pleasant ambiance, it's a perfect place for students to relax and socialize.

Cafeteria Seating
Food Counter

Computer Labs

Multiple computer laboratories equipped with the latest hardware and software cater to various academic requirements. High-speed internet connectivity and technical support ensure smooth operation for research and practical sessions.

Computer Lab
Digital Learning Space

Campus Tour

We offer guided tours of our campus to prospective students and visitors. The tour includes visits to academic buildings, library, laboratories, sports facilities, and other areas of interest. Campus tours are available on weekdays (Monday to Friday) from 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM.

To schedule a campus tour, please contact our administrative office at least two days in advance.
