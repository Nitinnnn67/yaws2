Alumni Interaction

Reminiscence - 2k19

In 2019 PRUTSIM arranged Alumni meet "Reminiscence" where students from past batches visited the campus. They all got nostalgic and lost in the memories of the good old days. They shared their work experiences and updated current batch students on how to prepare for the corporate world and what the expectations are from a management student in the professional environment.

Saketians Speak Your Mind

A unique competition was conducted for students of Saket Gyanpeeth where they chose to speak upon topics of their choice and expressed their views. The special element was that these students were judged by our own alumni, creating a joyful sharing of knowledge and thought processes. We invited alumni and felicitated them for being judges in this competition.
