From Placement Officer's Desk

The one of the core, focus and fundamental function in today's corporate world is Human Asset management with high expectation from the B schools is to fulfil their job requirement with trained & skilled management students.

We have robust placement process for our students to sustain in the competitive markets with the needs to perform with utmost quality and professionalism.

We have well defined, multi focused training & development plan during their academic years which ensures that the students get placed where they fit intellectually and culturally and the most important is right job fit that will be mutually beneficial.

We focus on training each student to offer right industry exposure & skill enhancement and sustain with corporate expectations.

During last 10 years many satisfactory recruiters regularly visit our campus and recruit our students, credit goes to our trained alumni performance and they consistently add value to their organizations and have moved up the ranks faster.

The Placement Cell is key department to play important role in connecting students with potential employers and facilitating the job placement process.

The cell takes charge of both on-campus and off-campus placement activities, to connect students and the industrial community for mutual Industry-Institution Interaction. The major responsibilities of the Placement Cell is to establish and maintain relationships with potential employers, includes reaching out to companies, recruiters, and hiring managers to ensure that students have access to a wide range of job opportunities.

Throughout the four semesters, the Placement cell conducts a series of placement and employability skills training programs for the students to face the corporate challenges for their future career.

The cell also coordinates on-campus recruitment events, such as job fairs, company visits, and campus interviews, providing students with the opportunity to meet and interview with potential employers and also provide career counselling and guidance to students with special services such as resume writing, interview preparation, and job search strategies.

Additionally, the cell also helps students with internships, industrial visits, live projects, and guest lectures to give them an opportunity to explore the real-time industry scenario.

Finally, the Placement Cell is responsible for monitoring the placement process and maintaining records to ensure transparency and fairness in the process.

Placement Guidelines

Placement and Career Guidance Centre

PRUSTIM aims at nurturing Industry – Institute interaction by organizing numerous activities that provide students a feel of professionalism. The changed economic scenario across the globe has forced organizations to look for professionals who will be early contributors to business functions, wherein one can help add value to the organization and be a role model of its future growth.

PRUSTIM enhance student's employability skills by:

  • Conducting intensive training to sharpen the Management Competencies of the students as required by the industry
  • Regular soft skill sessions including language improvement sessions
  • Regular Guest lectures from industry personnel for imparting practical view of the corporate world
  • Series of Mock interview and grooming sessions before the campus season
  • Corporate mentoring of the students by people from the industry to make them industry ready and acceptable

Career Guidance Steps:

  • Students undergo several self-assessments, to identify their personality and career preferences.
  • We then provide them with detailed information about various industries and career options.
  • Post assessment, we equip our students with extensive and most effective Performance Enhancing Techniques.
  • These techniques help enhance student's ability to learn and improve their overall performance both academically and professionally.
  • We have trained experts who teach such techniques and handle each student carefully. These sessions are tuned towards improving the mental performance and one's attitude towards learning.
