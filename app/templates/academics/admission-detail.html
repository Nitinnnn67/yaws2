M.M.S. Programme Admission Details

About the Programme

M.M.S. is a two year full-time master's degree course affiliated to the University of Mumbai. This course is approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi. The program is conducted under the directions of the University of Mumbai with specializations offered in the areas of:

  • Finance
  • Marketing
  • Operations
  • HR
  • Systems

Admission Process

The M.M.S. admission process is administered by the DTE (Directorate of Technical Education), Maharashtra. The process includes:

  • Online Computer Based Test MAH- MBA/MMS CET
  • Centralized Admission Process (CAP) conducted by CET Cell
  • Merit-based seat allocation

Number of Seats

120 seats available

Eligibility Criteria

  • Basic Qualification: Three-year Bachelor's Degree from UGC recognized Universities or AIU with minimum 50% marks in aggregate (45% for reserved categories from Maharashtra State)
  • OR: Final year students of any Bachelor's degree from UGC recognized Universities or AIU

Important Note for Reserved Categories

Candidates belonging to SC, VJ/DT (NT (A)), NT (B), NT(C), NT (D), OBC, SBC categories must produce:

  • "Caste Validity Certificate" issued by Scrutiny Committee of Social Welfare Department
  • ST category candidates must submit "Tribe Validity Certificate" issued by Scrutiny Committee of Tribal Department
  • Valid Non-Creamy Layer certificate (except SC, ST candidates)
