Ph.D. Programme in Management Studies

Doctoral-level Programme at PRUTSIM, University of Mumbai

Course Information

PRUTSIM offers doctoral-level programme, Ph.D. (Management studies) through its well recognized research center affiliated with the University of Mumbai. The Ph.D. programme is designed for students interested in management teaching, research, and consultancy.

Main Objectives of the Programme:

  • Develop management teachers well equipped with the skills required for imparting education to business school students
  • Develop bright young academicians with research bent of mind to conduct research in contemporary issues in management

Selection Procedure / Admission

Educational Qualification
Candidate should have Post Graduation in Business Management and related discipline of Management.
Entrance Examination
Candidate should qualify any of these Examinations: PET/NET/SET/GRF
Personal Interview
Candidate should have Personal Interview with your Research Proposal.

For other details refer VCD/947 2018 of University of Mumbai.
