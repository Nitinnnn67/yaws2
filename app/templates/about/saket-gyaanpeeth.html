About Saket Gyanpeeth

"GYANAM ANANTAM" - Knowledge is Infinite

Delivering quality education for over 29+ years

Saket Gyanpeeth was established in the year 1994 with "GYANAM ANANTAM" as its motto. The main purpose of the trust is to render selfless service to the society through value-based education. The noble concept of service to humanity: service to God has truly inspired our founder Chairman Dr. Ram Ujagar Tiwari, under whose guidance Saket Gyanpeeth is promoting better cause of education in Kalyan, Maharashtra and other remote areas as well.

The Trust provides medical facilities to needy and deserving persons by organizing camps from time to time and encourages all-round development of the future generation by giving numerous scholarships and free ships to brilliant and needy students.

2007

Started Saket College of Education (B.Ed) affiliated to University of Mumbai.

Nursing Institute

Extended social responsibility by starting Saket Institute of Nursing.

Ph.D. Research Center

Received approval for Ph.D. Research Center in Management.

Today, Saket College of Arts, Science and Commerce, affiliated to University of Mumbai, provides education leading to B.A, B.Com, B.M.S., B.A.F., B.B.I., B.I.M., B.F.M., B.Sc(Computer Science), B.Sc (I.T.), M.Com ( Advanced Accountancy), M.Sc (I.T.) and M.A. (Hindi).

We have also established "Saket Institute of Professional Training and Skill Development" to offer courses and programs related to computer programming, communication skills, direct & indirect taxes, tours & tourism, logistics supply chain management, retail management, and more.

We are proud to have received permanent Hindi Linguistic Minority status from the Government of Maharashtra and we continue to expand our educational offerings to serve the community better.
